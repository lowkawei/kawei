Talkin’ short film in Melbourne

Q. I’ve read that you’ve screened over 700 short films since you started. How do you source the films?

Sometimes I’m on the prowl for films…other times they’re sent in to us…

Q. Amongst In the Realm of the Senses , Tropfest, St Kilda Film Festival and Resfest, where do you think Fitzroy Shorts fits in?

I think we have a fantastic venue. It’s a relaxed environment and an unusual space to screen in. It differs from the normal film entertainment venues around town – it has a buzz to it.

It’s a regular, monthly event on the film calendar as opposed to other film events, which are once off or annual. We’ve managed to garner quite a lot of publicity because of our regularity.

Q. The St Kilda Film Festival is one of Melbourne’s longest running short film events, how do you feel about the fact that some of the films screened there had their Victorian premier at Fitzroy Shorts?

I feel over the moon with the prospect of being allowed to screen films without other events imposing their strict premier screening policies on us. It’s about screening films and giving filmmakers the opportunity to screen their films as many times as possible without restrictions. >>>
