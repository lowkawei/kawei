Comics come to life

By Melanie Sheridan

Making a movie based on a comic book is a difficult art. The film medium necessitates a certain realism and the comic page demands the opposite. Technology has made the bridge between the comic book page and the screen easier to cross, but spectacular visuals alone do not make a movie. As any fan will tell you, they are not called comic books for nothing; sure there’s plenty of KAPOW! but there’s equally as much emphasis on character development. So with the flood of comic book films spreading, like a swarm of mutant beasts across the earth, Melanie Sheridan separates the good guys from the bad >>>
