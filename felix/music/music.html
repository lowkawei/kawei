Burn down the record store — buying rare music online

By David Winter

Gone are the days of sifting through racks at the record store, looking for the ultimate rare album. Shopping online for rare music is increasingly popular because it is easy, fast and allows you to access a global marketplace. Just be careful you don't get burnt – online prices are often exponentially higher than those in record stores.
